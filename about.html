who we are

A Bunch of Young Engineers

We like to think of ourselves as a unique group of interaction designers and strategists who design and develop websites and web applications that help guarantee the rapid success of your product.

Our team has the creative flair and technical expertise to deliver innovative websites and web applications with a unique user experience and an original exquisiteness setting your product apart from the rest of the crowd.

We create clean, fresh websites and web applications and always put the user experience first. Most importantly, we use the latest web technologies and services to produce cost-effective solutions.

How we work

Agile, agile and agile

Agile Process

Not all projects need a rigid plan or need to be cookie cutter. Our Design process allows us to shortcut to the items that provide the most value for a business. We'll customize our projects to get you the best end result based on our three phases: Define, Frame and Build.

T-Shaped

Our team is composed of designers who can write code and design visuals, engineers who can build interfaces and marketers who understand programming. We're not too shabby at writing either. This is beneficial for creating faster turnarounds, reducing problems and focusing ideas.

User Centric

VERGEVISION loves working with customers and users on a regular basis to test our designs. Whether it's through direct contact in usability tests or remote concept testing through Verify, we use our findings to inform our design work.

No Project Managers

VERGEVISION is focused on creating direct conversations with our clients and the people that actually do the work. We train our Designers and Design Leads to manage projects so that you have direct access to the people who understand the problem. Shorter timelines happen as a result.

Design for people

We're a team of product designers who help companies design for people. And we have fun while we're doing it!

We're not just a web design agency. We're not just a graphic design firm. We're not just coders and we're not just artists. We're a team of T-shaped product designers who help grow businesses. (Oh, and we're not just dudes and dudettes with expensive chairs, either.)

We want our idea to be accessible

Our team talent means everything to us—it defines our culture, process and environment. We put an emphasis on working with smart people who uphold our values and create new opportunities for our clients, customers and users.

We present ourselves to our clients in the same way we do to our employees, vendors and potential candidates. We're passionate about what we do and we want to let everyone get a glimpse of what makes ZURB a great place to work. It's not a one-time pitch—it's how we look at every work day.
